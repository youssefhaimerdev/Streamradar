FAQ

Common Questions

What channels and services does StreamRadar cover?

All major US broadcast networks (ABC, NBC, CBS, Fox, The CW), 100+ cable channels (ESPN, TNT, TBS, AMC, FX, Bravo, USA, HGTV, and more), plus every major streaming service: Netflix, Hulu, Disney+, Max, Peacock, Paramount+, Apple TV+, Amazon Prime Video, ESPN+, and YouTube TV.

Is StreamRadar completely free?

Yes — 100% free, no account needed. StreamRadar earns revenue through affiliate partnerships. If you click and subscribe, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.

How accurate is the schedule information?

StreamRadar uses real-time AI to surface the most current schedule data. For live sports, schedules are accurate as of search time. We always recommend verifying the exact start time directly with your provider, especially for playoff events.

Can I search for international sports?

Yes. StreamRadar covers Premier League (NBC/Peacock), La Liga (ESPN+), Bundesliga, Champions League, Formula 1, cricket, rugby, and many more international sports.

What if the game is blacked out in my area?

StreamRadar flags potential blackouts and suggests streaming alternatives like ESPN+, FuboTV, or YouTube TV that offer out-of-market or reduced-blackout options.

Does StreamRadar work for on-demand content too?

Yes — both live broadcasts and on-demand. Whether you want tonight's game on cable or a show to binge on Netflix, we'll point you to the right place.
